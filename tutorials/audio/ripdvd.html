Rip a Scene from a DVD

A. Insert DVD
  • Figure out the scene number(s) you need

B. Quit DVD Player

C. Ripping the DVD
  1. Open Handbrake
  2. Select your DVD
  3. Choose the “VIDEO_TS” folder
  4. Click Open
  5. Select the chapter(s) your scene is in.
  6. Press Start
  7. Your video will be created on the desktop


D. Converting (Skip if your video is .mp4)
  1. Open Encoder
  2. Drag and drop your video into the Queue Pane
  3. Click the yellow link below “Preset” in the Queue Pane
  4. Change the format to H.264
  5. Click OK
  6. Click the play button


E. Import your video into Pro Tools
