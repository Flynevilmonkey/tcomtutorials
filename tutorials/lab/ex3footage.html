Transfer Footage From EX-3 with XD-Cam Transfer

  1. Plug in the card reader to an outlet and the computer and insert the SxS card.
  2. Open the XD-Cam Transfer utility on the Mac. (In the Applications folder)
  3. To set the scratch disk, click on XDCAM Transfer in the upper menu bar
    • Go to Prefernces
    • Select the Import tab
    • Choose the directory you want to use.
  4. Select all the clips you want to import (Cmd-A for all) and click Import at the bottom right corner.
  5. Wait for all the imports to complete eject and your're done!
