Letterman and Clone Audio Bay Troubleshooting

Restart the Computer

The gear is off

  • Make sure the power switch on all the gear is on
  • The switch is on the bottom right of all four racks

Check the CO3

  • The one on the left should be set to AES/BDU
  • The one on the right should be set to S/PDIF

Make sure the preamps is on

  • The power button is on the left side

Check that the Mbox on top of the computer is light

  • Get the Lab Technician in BC194G
